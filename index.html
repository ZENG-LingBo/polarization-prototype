🎤 DefuseLab — Study 1

A prototype of the K-pop rival-fandom platform testing whether a shared-activity feature (Collab Spotlight) reduces cross-fandom toxicity. Two separate entrances below — the participant app and the researcher dashboard are deliberately kept apart (blinding).
📱

I'm a participant →

Open the mobile kfeed app: a K-pop community feed. Browse, react, post, and take part in a collab. Takes a few minutes.
📊

I'm a researcher →

Open the passphrase-gated dashboard: per-arm toxicity (C0/C1/C2), the mechanism metrics, and each measure's source paper. Not visible to participants.
Status & ethics. This build is for instrument-building / internal testing. The app records behavioral data, so a real recruited run is IRB-gated (see PLAN.md §11). Rival pair is ARMY (BTS) × BLINK (BLACKPINK) (see FANDOM_SELECTION.md); seeded posts are anonymized & paraphrased, exclude member-targeting content, and are not attributed to real individuals. Measures are anchored to a model paper (GuesSync!, CSCW 2023) — grounding in MEASURES.md / METRICS.md. Backend deploy: backend/ (runs fully offline until then).
